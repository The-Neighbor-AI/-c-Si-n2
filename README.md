- **Material**: 9N-Pure Silicon (Single Crystal)

    - **Physical function**: Storage of torsional stress energy during thermal expansion.

- **Negative Thermal Expansion (NTE) Mechanism**:
    - Asymmetric coating of $SiO_2$ (2-5nm thickness) on silicon arms.
    - Under operational heat ($60^\circ\text{C}$ to $80^\circ\text{C}$), the differential in thermal expansion coefficients induces a "twisting" motion, reflecting heat noise as directional phonon transport.
